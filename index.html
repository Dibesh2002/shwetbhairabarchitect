About Us

Shwet Bhairab Architects & Associates (SB Architects & Associates) is a sole proprietorship architectural firm, established in 2068/05/11, with a mission to provide professional and economical solutions without compromising quality. We offer consultancy services to individuals, institutions, and government sectors across a range of disciplines.

Our office is located at Madhyapur Thimi-15, Bhaktapur, Nepal, equipped with modern facilities and a skilled team of professionals.
