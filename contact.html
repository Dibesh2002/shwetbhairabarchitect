Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical process for working with your firm?

Our process typically begins with an initial consultation to understand your needs and vision. We then move through concept design, design development, construction documentation, and construction administration phases. Throughout the process, we maintain open communication and collaboration with our clients.

How much does it cost to hire an architect?

Architectural fees vary depending on project size, complexity, and scope of services. We offer flexible fee structures including fixed fees, percentage of construction costs, or hourly rates. We're happy to discuss your specific project and provide a detailed fee proposal.

How long does the architectural design process take?

The timeline varies depending on project size and complexity. A typical residential project might take 3-6 months for design and documentation, while larger commercial projects can take 6-12 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work on projects outside your local area?

Yes, we work on projects throughout the region and have experience managing remote projects. We use digital tools for communication and collaboration, and we travel as needed for site visits and key meetings.
